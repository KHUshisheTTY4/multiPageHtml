BIRIYANI

Description

Biryani is one of the most popular dishes in South Asia, as well as among the diaspora from the region

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • onion
  • Tomato
  • ghee
  • curd
  • whole spices
  • biriyani masala
  • chilli

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with curd and spices
  2. Prepare basmati rice seperately i.e boiled to 90%
  3. add ghee to cooking utensil
  4. add onion,spices,tomato,chilli,masala and marinated chicken
  5. Add rice
  6. cook for 20 mins
